Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
3. Do not click on suspicious links or emails.
4. Always verify the sender before sharing information.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or transactions.
6. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
7. Check for secure websites (https) before entering details.
8. Regularly update your devices and applications.
9. Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in.
10. Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud.
